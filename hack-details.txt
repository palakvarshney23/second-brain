WHAT A GOOD SUBMISSION LOOKS LIKE
Your model is dumb. Your job is to engineer around its weaknesses. Pick the techniques that fit your project:
Structured Prompts
Constrain output to what the model can handle. Shape the
input, shape the result.
RAG
Model doesn't need to know — just read and respond.
Retrieval does the heavy lifting.
Tool Use
Orchestrate actions, don't generate raw knowledge. Let
tools do what models can't.
Multi-Step Pipelines
Break tasks into tiny subtasks. Small models excel at
narrow, well-defined jobs.
Validation Layers
Catch bad outputs and retry. Build the safety net your
model needs.
Caching & Fine-tuning
Pre-compute what you can. Make a tiny model an expert at
one specific thing.
Show the judges you thought about it. Not a benchmark wrapper. Not a chatbot that just chats.
